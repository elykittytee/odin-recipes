Coffee

Description, from Wikipedia

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain flowering plants in the Coffea genus. From the coffee fruit, the seeds are separated to produce a stable, raw product: unroasted green coffee.

This recipe will be for making fresh ground coffee with a drip maker. Everyone's personal coffee preferences are unique. Not every coffee cup is made the same.

Ingredients

  • water
  • cream of choice
  • sweetener of choice
  • coffee beans, roasted and whole

You will also need:

  • a coffee grinder
  • drip coffee maker
  • filter for coffee maker (check the manufacturer)
  • a tablespoon
  • a handheld milk frother
  • a vessel for your finished coffee

Personal Preferences: Cream & Sugar are substituted with condensed milk.

Recipe Directions

Prepare your Coffee and Coffee Maker

  1. Spoon whole, roasted coffee beans into grinder.
    • Ratio is about 1 tbsp per cup of coffee
  2. Grind to desired size. For a drip maker, medium to medium fine is preferable.
  3. Insert your filter into the basket of your coffee maker. Pour beans into the filter.
  4. Spoon desired amount of cream and sugar into your desired coffee vessel.
  5. Put water into the drip coffee maker, use the numbers to determine how much water you need per cup.
    • A standard "cup" is not a true cup measurement and is about 5-6oz of water.
  6. Make sure you have the right coffee vessel (whether it's your cup or a carafe) in the coffee maker to catch the precious coffee.
  7. Press the button.

Combine your Coffee

  1. If using a carafe: Pour the coffee into your desired coffee vessel.
  2. If coffee was made into your personal cup: see next.
  3. Using the milk frother, mix the coffee and your cream & sugar.

Enjoy your coffee~
